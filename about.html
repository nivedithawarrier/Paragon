About Paragon

Our chef cooks the most delicious food for you

The Indian cuisine restaurant is blessed with contemporary dishes which uphold a history of classical Indian heredity. We grant impeccable dining experience for our visitors. The total ambience of the restaurant maintains the balance between excellent service and mouth watering food items.

After initially offering a fusion of the Malabar region's Moplah and Thiyya Cuisines, the master chefs at Paragon have ceaselessly innovated to transform the cuisine over the years. We hope you have a Paragon experience filled with unmatched taste, delightful contentment and memories to cherish. Bon Appetit
